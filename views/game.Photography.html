Welcome to the photography game. In this game your goal is to try to survive as long as possible. Do not let your Working Capital drop below -$2000.

You can end this tutorial at any time. Click next to continue the tutorial.

Close

Here are your financial status.

Cash at bank measures the amount of money you have.

Working Capital is your actual value.

Current Assets is the value of all your assets together (photos etc...).

Current Liabilities is your monthly debt you must pay.

Close

You must travel and take photos of locations and events scattered around Australia.

Each of the red marker on the map indicates a potential for photos to be taken.

Hover over them to see a overview of the area.

Close

Potential Revenue is the amount of money you could make from that area.

Travel Expense is the cost of travelling to that location from your current location. This will deduct from your cash at bank.

Close
