War is a constant threat to humanity. Titled as one of the “four horsemen”, it is capable of great suffering and destruction. In this day and age, in a country with little experience of the tragedy of wartime, STAB Inc. believes that it is important for the new generations to have a realistic view on the concept of war and to be aware of the true scale of death, not simply as a statistic but also on a personal level. With exception, a large portion of popular media has portrayed war as heroic, glorified and masochistic especially in a historic scenario, when the reality is, you are more likely to die in war, meaninglessly, alone and forgotten by most.
